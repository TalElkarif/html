Beitar Jerusalem Football Club is an Israeli football club based in the city of Jerusalem, that plays in the Israeli Premier League, the top tier in Israeli football.
The club has traditionally worn kit colours of yellow and black.
The team has played its home matches in Teddy Stadium. The stadium is the largest stadium in Israel with a capacity of 34,000.
The club is one of the most popular in Israel and is among the Israeli clubs with the highest number of fans in the country.
